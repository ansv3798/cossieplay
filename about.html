WHAT IS COSPLAY?

Cosplay is the combination of the words “costume” and “role-play”. It is used to describe dressing up as a character from games, movies, anime, comics and more. Cosplay is a pastime enjoyed by young and old and Oz Comic-Con celebrates this community with showcases, competitions and more! Coming in cosplay to Oz Comic-Con? Be sure to read our Cosplay Props policy! Cosplay Central is a dedicated space on the show floor set up specifically for the cosplay community and friends! Featuring cosplay special guests, local costume displays, a Cosplay Photography Gallery, cosplay photographers, Cosplay Change Room & Repair Station and more! This year, Oz Comic-Con is excited to welcome Spotlight and Brother as the Presenting Partners of Cosplay Central 2017. Find out more about the partnership here. If you have any questions about cosplay please contact Michelle.

ANTI-HARASSMENT POLICY

Oz Comic-Con has a ZERO TOLERANCE POLICY for harassment of any kind. Remember: Cosplay is not consent. Keep your hands to yourself. If you would like to take a picture with or of another Oz Comic-Con Fan, always ask first and respect that person's right to say no. When at Oz Comic-Con, be respectful, be nice, be cool and be kind to each other.
