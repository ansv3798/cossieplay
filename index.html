Cosplay – Hulkstown 2017

Are you a step above the parade but not quite ready for the championship? Do you have a skit to perform or want to tell the audience about your costume? Then this is the competition for you! This event requires you to sign up online – no on-the-day sign-ups or additions will be taken. This event will take place on the Saturday of Oz Comic-Con. The Supanova Cosplay Competition presented by Madman Entertainment is always the highlight of a Supanova weekend for so many people. It’s time to finish off your amazing costume, put together your best performance and make that stage your own as you compete for amazing prizes. Our cosplay judges will be looking for the best from all our entrants and rewarding those who stand above the rest. In 2017 we welcome our new category sponsor Ironlak, who will be supporting our ‘Saturday Best in Show’ through the year with an amazing $1,000 voucher on their range of incredible paints and other art supplies.

Best in Show: Overall, the standout costume from the entire competition!

Best Cosplay: Combining Character Portrayal, and Costume Quality!

Best Craftsmanship: For those dedicated to the craft that is Costume Making!

Best Performance:Entertaining, Engaging or Funny – this is a show after all!

Best Group: Two or More great costumes together, always amazing to see!

Judges Award: Some costumes deserve recognition, even if they don’t fit other categories!

Best parade: team work, should be rewarded!

Pretties Award: the public will choose the hottest in the room!

CODE OF CONDUCT

We do not tolerate harassment, bullying, or assault of participants in any form. Sexual language and imagery is not appropriate for any venue, including talks. Participants violating these rules may be sanctioned or expelled from the event without a refund at the discretion of the organisers, or authorities may be contacted.
